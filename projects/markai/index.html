What is MarkAI?

MarkAI is a lightweight, structured language designed for embedding AI instructions inside Markdown-like documents.

It allows for **machine-executable AI commands** while maintaining full human readability.

Example Syntax

With MarkAI, you can embed AI-powered automation directly into your documents:
